Removing a credit card associated with your user account

  • mac
  • windows
  • linux
  • all

If you're paying for GitHub with a coupon, you can remove your credit card information from GitHub.

Warning: Removing a credit card takes effect immediately. If you remove a credit card without downgrading your paid account, you may find yourself locked out.

If you switch your payment to Paypal or downgrade your account to free, we'll also automatically remove your credit card.

  1. In the Billing Information section, click .
  2. Confirm the change by clicking Remove credit card.
