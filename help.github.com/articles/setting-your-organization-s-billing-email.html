Setting your organization's billing email

  • mac
  • windows
  • linux
  • all

Your organization's billing email is where GitHub sends receipts, credit card or PayPal charges, and other billing-related communication.

Only members of the Owners team can access billing information on the account.

  1. In the top right corner of any page, click your username.

  2. On the left side of your profile page, under "Organizations", click the icon for your organization.

  3. To the right of your organization name, click .

  4. In the Settings sidebar, click Billing.

  5. Under Billing Email, type a valid email address. The email does not need to be unique to the organization account.
