Monthly and Yearly Billing

  • mac
  • windows
  • linux
  • all

Choose to make payments on a monthly or yearly billing cycle, depending on your needs.

Switching between monthly and yearly billing

If you decide to switch to yearly billing from a monthly billing plan, or vice-versa, our billing system automatically prorates your account to compensate for the price difference by charging or refunding the difference for the remainder of the billing year.

For more information on how your billing is calculated depending on your new plan type, see "How does upgrading or downgrading affect the billing process?"

Changing your personal account billing plan

  1. In the top right corner of any page, click .

  2. In the Billing overview section, click Change plan.

  3. At the top right corner of the plans table, click Switch to monthly billing or Switch to yearly billing.

Changing your organization billing plan

Warning: To prevent unwanted charges, double check to make sure that you are upgrading the correct account before you change your billing plan.

  1. In the top right corner of any page, click your username.

  2. On the left side of your profile page, under "Organizations", click the icon for your organization.

  3. In the Settings sidebar, click Billing.

  4. Under "Billing overview", click Change plan.

  5. At the top right corner, click Switch to monthly billing or Switch to yearly billing.
