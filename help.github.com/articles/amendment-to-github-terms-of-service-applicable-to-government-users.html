This Amendment to GitHub's Terms of Service applies only to users of GitHub's sites or services that are accessing or using or wish to access or use GitHub sites or services in their capacity as government or public entities or in their capacity as employees of a government or public entity. If you are a government or public entity, read on. This Amendment applies to you. If you aren't, then GitHub's Terms of Service apply to you.
