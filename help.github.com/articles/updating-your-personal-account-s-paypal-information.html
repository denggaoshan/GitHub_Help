Updating your personal account's PayPal information

  • mac
  • windows
  • linux
  • all

You can update the PayPal account that we use to bill your account at any time.

  1. Click Remove PayPal account.
  2. Review your choice and click Remove PayPal account.
  3. Under "Pay with," select PayPal account.
  4. Click PayPal.
  5. Log into PayPal with your account information.
  6. Agree to the terms of service for paying with PayPal.
  7. Select your country of residency, as well as your state or province (if you're asked).
  8. Type your postal code.

  9. If you're a European business, enter your VAT ID.

  10. Click Process payment to finish upgrading your account.

If you have any private repositories, you should make sure to add your new PayPal billing information. Otherwise, you are at a risk of having your personal account locked.
